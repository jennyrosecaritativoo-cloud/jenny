Our Academic Strands

Empowering Students through Specialized Subject Matter

STEM

Science & Technology

Focuses on advanced mathematics and scientific concepts. Designed for students curious about innovation, research, and engineering.

Career Path:

  • Engineer / Architect
  • Medical Professional
  • Data Analyst

ABM

Business & Finance

Concentrates on financial management and accounting. Perfect for future leaders and entrepreneurs.

Career Path:

  • Accountant (CPA)
  • Business Manager
  • Entrepreneur

HUMSS

Humanities & Arts

Explores human behavior and social issues. Builds critical thinking for those pursuing law or social work.

Career Path:

  • Lawyer / Politician
  • Journalist / Teacher
  • Psychologist

GAS

General Academics

Provides a broad overview of all academic tracks. It is ideal for students who want a flexible curriculum while preparing for various college degrees.

Career Path:

  • Public Administrator
  • Education Specialist
  • Human Resources

TVL - ICT

Digital Technology

A hands-on subject focusing on web development and digital design. Provides immediate technical skills for the 21st-century.

Career Path:

  • Web Developer
  • System Administrator
  • Graphic Designer
